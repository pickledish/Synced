Create a Firekey

Firekey is a temporary, collaborative notepad that's designed to be easy to share. Think of it like a Google Doc that's editable by anyone who has the link, except that link isn't so long it would take 30 seconds to say out loud.


When you click the "create" button down below, you'll be given an empty note along with a short English word, and if anyone visits www.firekey.club/thatword in a web browser (even on phones), they can edit that note with you in real-time. Since the key is just a word, it's easy to share with people around you when you can't copy-and-paste the link, like on a blackboard or in a grocery store.


Each Firekey (and its corresponding note) will last for 24 hours after it's created, then self-destruct.


The inspiration for this website came from ShoutKey, which does a similar thing to shorten long URLs.
